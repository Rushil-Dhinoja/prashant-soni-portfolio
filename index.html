My Story

Prashant Soni an engineer turned photographer started his photography career as a hobby. When he started out in photography he saved money from his pocket money and bought himself an old camera which, then he used to click photos of friends, family and natural. Within no time he started out taking professional orders for weddings and parties.

Today Prashant Soni is a professional photographer with experience of more than 2 years and has covered more than 100 weddings and parties. He is passionate, professional and enthusiastic for his job. He is a person who doesn't shut the camera until he gets the perfect click. He is also a videographer and has covered more than 20 pre-wedding shoots.
